AI-Assisted Tele-ECG Platform

What is Hearty Pro?

A multi-tenant B2B platform for healthcare institutions that perform AI-supported remote electrocardiogram reporting.

Main Features

  • Manual ECG PDF upload
  • Medical reporting with AI-assisted diagnosis
  • Simple signature for final reports
  • Usage and KPI dashboards
  • User, device, and organizational structure management
  • SaaS multi-tenant administration
